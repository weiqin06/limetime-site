Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026 · Last updated: June 21, 2026

LimeTime ("the App," "we," "us") is a family time-management app that lets a parent or guardian run a "time bank" for their children — depositing, withdrawing, and tracking screen-time as a virtual balance. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices. We have designed LimeTime to collect as little data as possible.

Who controls the data

The App is operated by the parent/guardian who creates the account ("Account Owner"). The Account Owner is responsible for any information they enter about their children. Children do not create their own accounts and do not provide us information directly.

Information we collect

Account information (provided by the parent):

  • Email address and password, used to create and secure your account.
  • A family name and an optional parent PIN (the PIN is stored only as a one-way hash).

Child profile information (entered by the parent about their child):

  • A display name or nickname you choose, an avatar (emoji), a color theme, and an optional kid passcode (stored only as a one-way hash).
  • We do not ask for a child's real name, birth date, photo, address, phone number, or precise location. Please do not enter a child's full legal name or other sensitive identifiers.

Activity data you create in the App:

  • Time-bank balances and transactions (deposits, withdrawals, timer sessions), routines, contracts/tasks, and time requests.
  • In-app notifications generated by these activities.

Device & technical information:

  • If you enable notifications, a push notification token (via Expo) and your language preference, used solely to deliver reminders and alerts to your devices.
  • Optional hardware "device tokens" you create to connect a physical timer; these are stored as hashed secrets.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or browsing history. The App contains no third-party advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

How we use information

  • To provide the core service: authenticate you, store your family's time-bank data, and sync it across your devices.
  • To send notifications you enable (e.g., a request was approved, a timer ended).
  • To secure the service and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Where data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase, a cloud backend provider, on secure infrastructure. Data is protected in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and access is restricted by per-account security rules so that one family cannot access another family's data. Push notifications are delivered through Expo's push service.

Children's privacy

LimeTime is intended to be set up and operated by a parent or guardian. Information about children is entered by, and under the control of, the parent. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Because the App is operated by the parent, the parent may review, edit, or delete their children's profiles at any time within the App. If you believe a child has provided us information directly without parental involvement, contact us and we will delete it.

Your choices and rights

  • Access & edit: You can view and edit your family and child profiles at any time in the App.
  • Delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from within the App at Parent → Settings → Delete account. This erases your family, all child profiles, balances, history, contracts, routines, and tokens, and cannot be undone.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, the associated data is removed from our active systems.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, an in-app notice.
