Common questions

How do I delete my account and data?
In the app: Parent → Settings → Delete account. This permanently erases your family and all of its data and cannot be undone.

Is my children's data private?
Yes. LimeTime has no ads and no third-party tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.

常见问题

如何删除我的账户和数据？
在应用内：家长 → 设置 → 删除账户。此操作会永久删除您的家庭及全部数据，且无法撤销。

我孩子的数据是否私密？
是的。LimeTime 没有广告，也没有第三方跟踪。详情请见我们的隐私政策。
